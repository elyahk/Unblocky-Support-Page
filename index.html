FAQ

Common questions

QHow does Unblocky actually block apps?
Unblocky uses Apple's official Screen Time / FamilyControls framework — the same system built into iOS parental controls. When you select an app to block, Unblocky applies a "shield" through Screen Time that intercepts every launch attempt. This requires you to grant Unblocky Screen Time authorization during onboarding.
QWhy does Unblocky need camera access?
The camera is used solely to count your push-up reps in real time using Apple's Vision framework for pose detection. The camera feed is processed entirely on-device and is never saved, stored, or transmitted anywhere. You can revoke camera access in Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera at any time.
QPush-up detection isn't working — what should I do?
For best results: place your phone on the floor in front of you in landscape or portrait orientation, ensure there's good lighting, and start with your full body visible in frame. The camera needs a clear view of your upper body as you move up and down. If detection is still off, try adjusting the phone position or improve lighting conditions.
QDoes Unblocky require an account?
No account, no sign-up, no email required. Download and start using Unblocky immediately. All your data — streaks, stats, settings — lives locally on your device.
QWhy does Unblocky need Screen Time authorization?
Screen Time authorization (via Apple's FamilyControls framework) is what gives Unblocky the ability to block and unblock apps. Without it, iOS will not allow any third-party app to control Screen Time. This permission stays entirely on your device — Unblocky cannot access Screen Time remotely.
QWhich apps can I block?
You can block any individual app installed on your device, or block entire categories (social networking, games, entertainment, etc.). You can also unblock or change your selection at any time from the Unblocky app.
QWhat happens after I earn my screen time?
Once you complete your push-up challenge, the blocked app unlocks for your earned duration (e.g., 10 minutes on Balanced mode). When that time expires, the app automatically re-locks. You'll need to do another push-up challenge to unlock it again.
QCan I change the difficulty or push-up requirement?
Yes. In Settings you can choose from Easy (3 reps → 5 min), Balanced (5 reps → 10 min), or Strong (10 reps → 15 min) presets, or configure a custom reps-to-minutes ratio. You can also set a maximum unlock cap.
QDoes Unblocky collect any personal data?
No. Unblocky does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No analytics, no crash reporting to external servers, no ads. All data stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
QHow do I completely reset Unblocky?
In Settings → Clear Data you can reset all stats and preferences. To remove all data entirely, delete the app from your device. Note: you may also need to manually remove Unblocky's Screen Time permissions in iOS Settings → Screen Time.
